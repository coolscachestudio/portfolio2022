A hospital grade UVC disinfection system, revolutionising the future of environmental disinfection.

The details

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, Cabrini Technology Group commissioned the design and development of a medical hospital-grade UVC disinfection system. We established Bluewave, a 'no touch' system revolutionising the future of environmental disinfection.

The problem

1/5

COVID-19 triggered unforseen events across the globe, and with it came a new wave of diligence and respect to maintaining hygiene in health and aged care facilities. Our challenge was to use mobile UV-light technology to construct an environmental disinfection system that:

  • reduces the spread of multi-drug resistant bacterial infections
  • is remotely controlled through a tablet-based application
  • is managed through a portal that covers room-by-room disinfection history and individual system usage

tablet application

User research

2/5

Due to working remotely, a strong emphasis was placed on secondary research from experts in the area. Research was broken up into market, technical and user research with members of staff from Cabrini Health regarding their current practices. Empathy mapping and user personas assisted me in deducing that the design needed to be:

  • intuitive and simple to account for users with low levels of English and/or technological capabilities — if the design was too complex, the user will refuse to use it
  • bold and clear to ensure the safety of users and those outside of the room being disinfected

user persona

Documenting the flow

3/5

I used these findings to create a service blueprint to illustrate the touchpoints between the user, tablet application, Bluewave device, and web portal. This included:

  • remotely setting and managing Bluewave disinfection cycles based on the room size and bacteria type via the tablet application
  • providing visual and audio feedback of cycles in progress, cycle interruptions, and completed cycles
  • a portal that displayed the status of all disinfected rooms that could be narrowed down into detailed summaries for reporting

service blueprint

Ideation + prototyping

4/5

As this project had a tight turnaround time, concepts were developed and iterated upon in a rapid manner. Over the course of three weeks, my designs morphed from basic wireframes to high fidelity prototypes in Figma based on consistent stakeholder and engineer feedback.
