A remote patient monitoring platform designed to securely capture and report PROMs and PREMs.

The details

Canberra Sleep Clinic struggled to engage with exisiting patients and attract new ones. We developed a scalable digital engagement tool that combined the management and retention of current patients, and the ability to interact with prospective ones.

Client

Canberra Sleep Clinic

Year

2020

Industry

Healthcare

Role

UI/UX Designer @ Medicity

The problem

1/5

The clinic's existing platform was outdated and offered limited opportunites to engage with patients. My challenge was to constuct an ecosystem comprising of two components - a mobile web application and administrator portal - that:

  • made it easier for healthcare professionals to better connect and engage with patients
  • allowed professionals to proactively approach patient management
  • improved operational efficiency to attain competitive advantage in the market

patient detail page

User research

2/5

I started by conducting a two day workshop with a small focus group of Stakeholders and key users in Canberra to gather insights into the clinic's current methods of patient retention and attraction. I undertook user interviews and tackled affinity mapping to further define their key users and to dictate current and future workflows.

With the deliverables set, I created a service blueprint to illustrate the touchpoints between the other applications they clinic would be using, the eCaptis platform, and patients mobiles.

service blueprint for exisiting customers

The specs

3/5

From this workshop, we set out a list of key deliverables

  • a desktop application that captured and reported on Patient Reported Outcome Measures (PROMs) and Patient Reported Experience Measures (PREMs) as a method of patient engagement
  • a desktop application that provided insights into patient health outcomes, the effectiveness of the care provided, and the overall patient experience
  • a mobile web application that implemented a QR based survey for prospective patients
  • an additional mobile web application that delivered Patient Reported Outcome Measures (PROMs) and Patient Reported Experience Measures (PREMs) to exisiting patients

Ideation + prototyping

4/5

As the project was proposed to be built off our existing proposal 'Modulus,' I was able to quickly produce and iterate concepts. This enabled me to produce hi-fidelty wireframes in a rapid pace as the client was largely satisifed with our exisiting proposal. Over the course of a month, I presented the client with multiple presentations over zoom to finely tweak features.

PROM + PREM survey creator

Success metrics

5/5

eCaptis has since been successfully deployed at Canberra Sleep Clinic, Cabrini Hospital, and Future implemention within other clinics and hospitals is pending; as if the development of additional features such and the Victorian Lung Cancer Registry. Additional features such as multilingual questionnaires and further graphing methods are in development.
